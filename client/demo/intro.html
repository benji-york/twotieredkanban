Welcome to the Valuenator Demo

In this demo version of Valuenator, you can work with tasks just as you would in the on-line version, except that:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser.
  • Therefore, you can't share tasks with other people.

Concepts

The goal of feature flow is to provide value as quickly as possible. Examples of value include enabling a customer to do something new, or more easily, increasing efficiency, or learning something about a customer or market

Boards

A board displays the features a team is working on. You can navigate to a board by clicking on it's name in the board menu, which you can view by clicking on the menu icon in the upper-left corner of the window. (You can get back here by clicking on the home icon in the menu.)

If you're an administrator, you can add a board from this menu.

Features

Features are the focus of team work.

At a high level, a board shows features, in the backlog, or in one of the feature states

Tasks
Features are implemented by one or more tasks.

Using

Drag feature and task cards between states.

Click on plus buttons to add features or tasks.

Click on pencil buttons to edit features.

Click on tasks to edit them.
